class - Table

attributes -
numPersons (integer)
numSeats (integer)
is_clean (boolean)
is_served (boolean)

add_remove_persons (adds or removes people from a table)
add_remove_seats (takes away a seat or adds a seat to a table, which means numPersons can be bigger or must be smaller)
clean_table (Changes is_clean to true and served to false)
serve_table (sets is_served to true and is_clean to false)

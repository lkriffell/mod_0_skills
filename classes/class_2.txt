class - Table

attributes -
num_persons (integer)
num_seats (integer)
is_clean (boolean)
is_served (boolean)

add_persons (adds people from a table depending on argument taken)
remove_persons (removes people from a table depending on argument taken)
add_seats (adds seats to a table)
remove_seats (takes away seats)
clean_table (Changes is_clean to true and served to false)
serve_table (sets is_served to true and is_clean to false)
